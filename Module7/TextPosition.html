A Day in the Sun

At first light the sun is at the horizon; a baseline of sorts. It is important to appreciate the moment the 1st light peeks out from the horizon. Take a moment and drink some H2O. It is the start of a new 1st day of the rest of your life.

The day brings ups and downs each day, but enjoy every moment so you have a positive day. The length of the day varies each day. The amount of sunshine we have varies each day.

To justify what we do each day, we need to think about what bring us joy and happines. Do not waste your day so it is at the bottom of your memory, but at the top of your experiences. To stay just in the middle is okay too, but may not be a memory that is super and at the top of mind.
